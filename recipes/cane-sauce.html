Cane Sauce

Description

Copy cat recipe of canes sauce from Raising Canes.

Indgredients

  • 1 Cup - Mayonnaise
  • 1/2 Cup - Ketchup
  • 1 Tablespoon - Worcestershire Sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon - Black Pepper
  • 1 Teaspoon - Cajun Seasoning
  • 1/2 Teaspoon - Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Add all of your indgredients to a large enough bowl
  2. Mix well
  3. Refridgerate
  4. Enjoy!
