Birria Tacos

Description

Trader Joe's Birria Tacos. All indgredients can be found at Trader Joe's

Indgredients

  • Trader Joe's Beef Birria - 1lb
  • Trader Joe's Shredded Mozzarella Cheese (Oaxaca) - 1lb
  • Trader Joe's Corn Tortilla - 12oz
  • White Onion
  • Lime
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Microwave Beef Birria - 2min
  2. Open seal and mix Birria then microwave again - 2.5min
  3. Dice your onions and cilantro into cubes
  4. Dip your tortilla into the birria then into the pan
  5. Add your beef and mozzarella cheese to tortillas
  6. Add your onions and cilantro to tortillas
  7. Fold and cook
  8. Squeeze some lime on
  9. Dip it in the left over birria
  10. Enjoy!
